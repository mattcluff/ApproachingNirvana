Band Bio

Approaching Nirvana started as an electronic/orchestral producing duo consisting of Sam Wilson and Andrew Sinclair in 2009. The two met each other on "NewGrounds" (an internet website that describes themselves as "A community of artists, game developers, musicians, voice actors and writers who create and share some of the best stuff on the web!"). The two eventually collaborated via Skype to create a song. It wouldn't be another 3 years until they met in person in Miama, Florida in 2012 to begin production of their music on a larger scale.

From 2012 to 2015 they produced 8 albums together. This was considered the golden era for the group and their YouTube Channel largely because of their easy going approach to allowing anybody to use their music on YouTube for their own videos so long as the band was credited properly. Several millions of views later and after several successful singles and albums, Sam Wilson left in 2016 to start his own label, but Andrew Sinclair continues to this day producing music under the Approaching Nirvana YouTube Channel. Ever since the group disbanded, their YouTube channel gets nowhere near the amount of video views as it did in it's prime.

Despite Approaching Nirvana's fading popularity, tens of thousands return each year to some of their greatest hits. Whether you love their high intensity pump-you-up songs like "Sugar High", beautiful voice collaborated tracks such as "Aim for Me", or some of their more chill music like "I Dream of Ibiza", you're bound to find out of their over 350 songs something that your ears will love.
